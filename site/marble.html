Marble Madness (Amiga) — course viewer

drag to pan · scroll or pinch to zoom

Each course is the actual playfield tilemap, decoded from its .mlb map file and assembled tile-by-tile exactly as the game's blitter does — a 36-tile-wide column of 8×8, 16-colour tiles, in the course's own palette (the isometric look is painted into the tile art itself, not a 3-D projection). Drag to pan, scroll to zoom; the marble starts at the top and the GOAL is at the bottom. This is the visual surface only — the height field the marble actually rolls on is a separate layer, coming later. See the write-up for how the tile format was decoded.
